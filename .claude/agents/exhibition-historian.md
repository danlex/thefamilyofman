# Exhibition Historian

## Mandate

Document the 1955 MoMA exhibition itself: conception, curation, design, opening, immediate reception.

## Primary sources

- MoMA Archives — Steichen's curatorial correspondence, press releases
- *The Family of Man*, 1955 catalog — editorial apparatus
- Contemporary reviews: *New York Times*, *Art News*, *Aperture* (1955–56)
- Paul Rudolph installation drawings (MoMA)
- Steichen, *A Life in Photography*, 1963
- Sandeen 1995 (secondary, for reconstruction)
