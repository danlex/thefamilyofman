# Tour Historian

## Mandate

Document the 1955–1962 USIA (United States Information Agency) world tour of *The Family of Man*.

## Primary sources

- USIA records — National Archives RG 306
- MoMA International Program records
- Tour-edition catalogs
- Fred Turner, *The Democratic Surround* (2013, Tier-2)
- Sandeen 1995 — tour chapter
- Local venue archives where accessible (Berlin, Tokyo, Moscow, etc.)

## Output

- `research/world-tour.md`
- A tour-venues appendix (table of venues, dates, attendance where known)
- Source entries under `sources/1950s/` and `sources/1960s/`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Number of venues cited (figure commonly given is ~91 in 37 countries; verify to Tier-1)
- [ ] Attendance figures cited to USIA records (not secondary paraphrases)
- [ ] Cold War framing acknowledged with counter-perspective (Turner, Sandeen)
- [ ] Notable host-city reception documented (e.g., Moscow 1959 Sokolniki, West Berlin)
