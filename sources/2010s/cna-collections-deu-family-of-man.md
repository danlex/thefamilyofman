Centre national de l'audiovisuel (CNA), Luxembourg. *The Family of Man / Familio de homo* (German-language collections page). https://steichencollections-cna.lu/deu/collections/1_the-family-of-man — accessed 2026-04-25.

## Relevance

The German-language counterpart to the CNA's English education portal (`src-cna-education`). This is the page on the institutional collections-website (steichencollections-cna.lu) that records the **donation, the permanent installation, and the second restoration phase** in concise, sourced form. Anchors three claims that are otherwise scattered or paraphrased across other sources:

1. The 1964 U.S.-government donation, made at Steichen's request
2. The 1994 inauguration of the permanent installation at Clervaux Castle
3. The 2010–2013 second restoration phase

## Key excerpts / pages

- "Die amerikanische Regierung schenkt Luxemburg auf Wunsch Edward Steichens die letzte vollständige Version der Wanderausstellung." (The American government donates the last complete version of the touring exhibition to Luxembourg at Edward Steichen's request.)
- "Präsentation der Sammlung als Dauerausstellung im Schloss von Clervaux" (Presentation of the collection as a permanent exhibition at Clervaux Castle.)
- "Eine zweite Restaurierungsphase wurde in den Jahren 2010-2013 unternommen." (A second restoration phase was undertaken in 2010–2013.)
