## Relevance

Named-author obituary of record for Ralph Morse (1917–2014), LIFE magazine photographer, whose print appears at plate #13 of *The Family of Man* (Lovers). Source for the biographical dates in `pher-ralph-morse`.

## Key excerpts / pages

- Birth: 1917, the Bronx, New York.
- Death: December 7, 2014, Delray Beach, Florida.
- Career summary notes his thirty-year tenure at *LIFE*, World War II combat coverage, and his reportage on the Mercury Seven astronauts.
