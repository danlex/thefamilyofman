## Relevance

Tier-1 institutional archive page for the Robert Capa Archive at ICP (founded by his brother Cornell Capa). Canonical reference for Robert Capa's dates (Oct 22, 1913 Budapest – May 25, 1954 Indochina), birth name (Endre Ernő Friedmann), and co-founding of Magnum Photos in 1947.

## Key excerpts / pages

- Birth: October 22, 1913, Budapest, Hungary (as Endre Ernő Friedmann).
- Death: May 25, 1954, Thai-Binh, Indochina / Vietnam.
- Nationality: Hungarian-American (per ICP scholarly convention).
- Co-founded Magnum Photos in 1947 with Henri Cartier-Bresson, David "Chim" Seymour, George Rodger, and William Vandivert.
