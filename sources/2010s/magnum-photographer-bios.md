## Relevance

Magnum Photos is the cooperative agency co-founded in 1947 by Robert Capa, Henri Cartier-Bresson, David "Chim" Seymour, George Rodger, and William Vandivert. Its photographer pages are the canonical institutional biographies for current and historical members. Several *Family of Man* contributors were Magnum members at the time of the exhibition (Capa #26, Rodger #35, Cartier-Bresson #31, Bischof #30, Haas #24, Erwitt #37/#50), and Wayne Miller joined Magnum later; their Magnum pages are cited for dates and nationality in `data/photographers.csv`.
