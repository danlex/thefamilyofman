## Citation

"Wynn Bullock, 73, Photographer Noted for Images of Nature." *The New York Times*, November 1975. Obituary notice reporting Bullock's death on November 16, 1975 at his home in Monterey, California. (Exact page and edition not re-verified in this seed entry — flagged for follow-up.)

## Relevance

Primary biographical reference for the birth year (1902) and death year (1975) of Wynn Bullock, whose print appears at plates #2 and #6 of *The Family of Man* (Prologue). Used for the `data/photographers.csv` row `pher-wynn-bullock`.

## Key excerpts / pages

- Death reported as November 16, 1975, at Monterey, California.
- Birth reported as April 18, 1902, Chicago, Illinois.
- Career summary notes his mid-career turn to photography (from music and law) and his association with the West Coast large-format tradition (Edward Weston, Ansel Adams).
