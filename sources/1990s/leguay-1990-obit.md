## Citation

"Laurence Le Guay." *The Sydney Morning Herald*, October 1990. Obituary notice for the Australian photographer Laurence Le Guay (1917–1990), co-founder of *Contemporary Photography* magazine (Sydney, 1946) and Australian commercial-photography pioneer.

## Key excerpts / pages

- Birth: 25 December 1916, Chatswood, Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. [CORRECTION: seed entry recorded 1917; corrected to 1916 per Wikipedia/independent cross-check — see Notes.]
- Death: 2 February 1990, Sydney.
- Career summary: Royal Australian Air Force service (WWII, aerial reconnaissance in North Africa); co-founder of *Contemporary Photography* (Sydney, first issue December 1946; ceased 1950); commercial-photography practice in Sydney.
