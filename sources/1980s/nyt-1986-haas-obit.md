## Citation

"Ernst Haas, Photographer, Dies at 65." *The New York Times*, September 1986. Obituary. (Haas died September 12, 1986 in New York City.)

## Relevance

Obituary of record for Ernst Haas (1921–1986), Magnum photographer whose print appears at plate #24 of *The Family of Man* (Lovers). Source for the biographical dates in `pher-ernst-haas`.

## Key excerpts / pages

- Birth: March 2, 1921, Vienna, Austria.
- Death: September 12, 1986, New York City.
- Career summary notes Haas's 1949 invitation to Magnum from Robert Capa, his 1951 LIFE essay "Images of a Magic City," and his role as an early pioneer of color reportage.
