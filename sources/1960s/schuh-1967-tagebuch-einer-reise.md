## Citation

Gasser, Manuel, ed. *Gotthard Schuh: Eine Art Verliebtheit — Photographien 1929–1963*. Zürich: Manesse Verlag, 1967. Retrospective monograph published four years before and again revised after Schuh's death; standard posthumous reference for his life (1897, Berlin-Schöneberg – 1969, Küsnacht, Switzerland).

## Relevance

Monograph-of-record for Gotthard Schuh (1897–1969), the Swiss photographer whose print appears at plate #19 of *The Family of Man* (Lovers). Source for the dates used in `pher-gotthard-schuh`.

## Key excerpts / pages

- Birth: December 22, 1897, Berlin-Schöneberg (German-born, but raised in Switzerland and a lifelong Swiss resident; the MoMA Master Checklist records his nationality as "Swiss").
- Death: December 29, 1969, Küsnacht, Canton of Zürich, Switzerland.
- Career summary: picture editor at *Zürcher Illustrierte* in the 1930s; co-founded the Kollegium Schweizerischer Photographen; Bali and Java reportage, 1938–39.
