## Citation

Genzlinger, Neil. "Frank Horvat, Innovative Fashion Photographer, Dies at 92." *The New York Times*, October 2020. Obituary.

## Relevance

Named-author obituary of record for Frank Horvat (1928–2020), whose print appears at plate #27 of *The Family of Man* (Marriage). Source for the biographical dates in `pher-frank-horvat`.

## Key excerpts / pages

- Birth: April 28, 1928, Abbazia, Kingdom of Italy (today Opatija, Croatia).
- Death: October 21, 2020, Boulogne-Billancourt, France.
- Career summary notes his early India assignment (1952–1955, the source of the *Family of Man* plate), his Black Star agency affiliation, and his reinvention of fashion reportage in the late 1950s using 35 mm cameras outdoors.
