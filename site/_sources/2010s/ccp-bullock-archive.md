## Relevance

Tier-1 institutional archive holding Bullock's papers, prints, and working records. The overview confirms the core biographical facts (Chicago birth 1902; Monterey death 1975) used in `pher-wynn-bullock`.

## Key excerpts / pages

- Archive overview lists Wynn Bullock as born 1902 in Chicago, Illinois, and died 1975 in Monterey, California.
- Archive covers the period of his photographic career from the 1940s onward, including the "Let There Be Light" and "Child in Forest" series often associated with the *Family of Man* Prologue plates.
