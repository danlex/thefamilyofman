The exhibition's mid-sequence cluster of household life, parenting, and young children follows the childbirth grouping in MoMA's own narrative summary of the flow.[^1] The CNA education portal lists both "family" and "childhood" among the exhibition's themes.[^2]

"Family and children" is a thematic cluster reconstructed from institutional summaries rather than a verbatim catalog heading. Because this research is partial (the 1955 catalog's interior pagination was not accessible during research on 2026-04-19), the cluster is provisional; photographers and individual photographs associated with it will be added as issue #1 populates `data/photographs.csv`.
