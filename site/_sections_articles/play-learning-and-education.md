"Education" is one of the themes CNA Luxembourg's education portal explicitly names in its account of the exhibition's 37 themes.[^1] Play, learning, and education are grouped together here as one cluster in the flow from childhood toward adulthood and work.

The grouping is a thematic cluster reconstructed from secondary summaries, not a verbatim heading from the 1955 catalog. Boundaries between "play" and "education" as exhibition themes differ across institutional accounts; our repo keeps them as one cluster and will re-parse if a primary catalog-page source resolves the boundaries.

[^1]: CNA Luxembourg, *The Family of Man, the book of humanity* — `src-cna-education`.
